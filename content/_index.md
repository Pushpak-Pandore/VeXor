## 🛡️ About Me

🚀 I'm a **Cybersecurity Engineer** with a focus on **Security Operations (SOC)** and **threat detection**.  

I have hands-on experience in **security monitoring, alert triage, and incident analysis** using tools like **Splunk, Rapid7, Cybereason, and ExtraHop**.  

🎯 I am **ISC² Certified in Cybersecurity (CC)** and currently preparing for **CEH v13**, aiming to strengthen both defensive and offensive security skills.
